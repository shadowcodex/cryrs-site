--- layout: posts title: "The Gallente Win The South" date: 2017-03-28 18:16:48 +0000 author: "Valindil89" categories: --- This month marks a glorious month indeed, as we joined with our brothers to liberate the south from the Caldari oppression. Together with Federation Uprising and fellow GalMil operatives we beat Black Shark Cult back and out of the south. Not only did we do it, we did it within a two week period. This marks one of the fastest blitzes ever performed by a militia.

As we continue to drive out the stragling Caldari survivors from the south, we enjoy the spoils of war and have very handy payouts from agents around the warzone.
